## Figure 3

**Figure 3. Sex-shared and sex-divergent mitochondrial transcriptional responses in AD.** Mitochondrial-restricted Yu analogue using the Phase 10 `core_mito` universe. Panel A displays stored highest- and lowest-similarity Zhang–Yu rank sets over nominal paired dimensions 162. Heatmap cells are observed paired-state occurrence counts; missing states are excluded rather than assigned to `(0,0)`. Row labels give the stored score and observed/nominal coverage; `*` marks stored directional BH FDR <= 0.05. Panel B uses stored Phase 11 one-sided ORA for Human MSigDB C2:CP v2026.1.Hs. GeneRatio = k/n, BackgroundRatio = M/N, and fold enrichment = (k/n)/(M/N); point x, size, and color show GeneRatio, overlap k, and within-query BH FDR. At most 15 pathways are displayed per query, and empty FDR-significant results remain explicit. Queries: Highest similarity (n=200, N=700; 0 FDR-significant; 0 displayed; 0 omitted); Lowest similarity (n=200, N=700; 81 FDR-significant; 15 displayed; 66 omitted). `all_mito_related` and MitoPathways are sensitivity profiles and are not used in this primary figure.
